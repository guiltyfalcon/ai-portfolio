Our Story

Founded by the Rossi family in 1985, The Golden Fork brings authentic Italian flavors to New York. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, using only the finest imported ingredients from Italy.

Every dish is prepared with love and attention to detail, creating an unforgettable dining experience that transports you straight to the streets of Rome.
