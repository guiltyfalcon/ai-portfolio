How to Customize Your Site

1

Download the Template

Click "View Demo" on any template, then save the page to your computer. All HTML and CSS is included.

2

Replace Content

Edit the HTML to change text, images, and contact information. Look for commented sections marking what to customize.

3

Set Up Contact Forms

Sign up for free at Formspree.io to get a form endpoint, then replace YOUR_FORMSPREE_ID in the HTML.

4

Deploy Your Site

Host for free on GitHub Pages, Netlify, or Vercel. No backend required – just upload your HTML files!
