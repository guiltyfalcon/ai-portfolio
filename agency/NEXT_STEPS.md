## Sales Process

### When a Lead Responds

1. **Send brief questionnaire** (link or copy/paste)
2. **Confirm package** and pricing
3. **Request 50% deposit** before starting
4. **Set expectations** on timeline

### Discovery Questions

```
1. What's your business/product?
2. Who's your target customer?
3. What's the main goal of this page? (signups, sales, leads)
4. Do you have any design preferences or examples you like?
5. What's your timeline?
6. Do you have logo/brand assets?
```
