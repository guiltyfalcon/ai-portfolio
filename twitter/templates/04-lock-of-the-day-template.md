# BetBrain AI - "Lock of the Day" Template

## Overview
Consistent daily format for showcasing the highest-confidence pick of the day. This becomes a signature series that followers look forward to.

| 🏈🏀⚾🏒 | Sport-specific |

- Use at start of lines for scannability
- Green heart 💚 occasionally for wins

## Posting Schedule

### Optimal Timing
| Sport | Best Post Time |
|-------|----------------|
| NFL | Thursday-Saturday (for Sunday games) |
| NBA | 10 AM - 12 PM ET (before tipoff) |
| MLB | 12 PM - 2 PM ET (before games) |
| NHL | 2 PM - 4 PM ET (before puck drop) |
| CFB | Thursday-Friday (for Saturday games) |

### Frequency
- **Ideal:** 1 lock per day (maintains exclusivity)
- **Maximum:** 2 locks (if exceptional value)
- **Minimum:** 4-5 per week (consistency matters)

### When NOT to Post a Lock
- No picks above 80% confidence
- Limited data/sample size
- Major injury uncertainty
- Line moving erratically (sharp vs. public confusion)

## Confidence Thresholds

### Lock of the Day Requirements
| Metric | Minimum | Ideal |
|--------|---------|-------|
| Confidence | 80% | 85%+ |
| Model Edge | 5% | 8%+ |
| Units | 2U | 3U+ |
| Data Points | 5+ | 10+ |

### Confidence Calibration
- **80-84%:** Strong play, 2-3U
- **85-89%:** Lock territory, 3-4U
- **90%+:** Rare, 4-5U max (never go all-in)
