### Bio Best Practices

#### DO:
✅ Lead with value proposition (first 50 characters are crucial)
✅ Include social proof (units won, win rate, followers)
✅ Show transparency commitment
✅ Add responsible gambling disclaimer
✅ Use 3-5 emojis max (scannable, not spammy)
✅ Include CTA with link

#### DON'T:
❌ Promise guaranteed wins (red flag + against TOS)
❌ Use excessive emojis (looks scammy)
❌ Write walls of text (hard to scan)
❌ Omit disclaimer (legal/ethical issue)
❌ Use vague claims ("best picks on Twitter")
