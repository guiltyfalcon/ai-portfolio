| Pick | Team/player + bet type |
| Odds | American odds (+150, -110) |

| Confidence | AI confidence % |
| Result | Win/Loss/Push |
| Profit/Loss | Units won/lost |
| Running Total | Cumulative units |

## Transparency Best Practices

### DO:
✅ Show all picks (wins AND losses)
✅ Include exact odds and units
✅ Track ROI, not just win rate
✅ Explain variance and downswings
✅ Update within 24 hours of results
✅ Keep historical records accessible

### DON'T:
❌ Hide or delete losing picks
❌ Cherry-pick only wins
❌ Exaggerate unit sizes after the fact
❌ Claim 100% accuracy (impossible)
❌ Move goalposts on results

## Engagement Tips

### For Daily Posts:
- Post within 2-4 hours of last game ending
- Use consistent format (recognizable at a glance)
- Highlight the day's best play
- Tease tomorrow's card

### For Weekly/Monthly Posts:
- Post on Sunday/Monday (weekly) or 1st of month
- Include visual charts/graphs when possible
- Share key learnings (shows growth mindset)
- Tag top performers if community-driven

### Call-to-Actions:
- "Follow for daily AI picks 🧠"
- "Drop a 🔥 if you're up this week"
- "RT to help a friend chase"
- "Track all picks: [link]"
